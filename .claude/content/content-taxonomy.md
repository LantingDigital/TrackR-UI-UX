# TrackR Content Taxonomy

10 categories, 70+ subcategories. This is the full editorial calendar for the TrackR content pipeline. Each subcategory implies a different research prompt template for Perplexity — build prompt templates per subcategory, not per top-level category.

Defined 2026-03-24 by Caleb.

---

## 1. News
- New Coaster Announcements (construction updates, manufacturer reveals, timeline tracking)
- Park Ownership & Business (mergers, acquisitions, earnings, executive changes)
- Ride Closures & Removals (permanent closures, SBNO status updates, relocation rumors)
- Regulatory & Safety (incident reports, new safety standards, ride inspection changes)
- Technology & Innovation (new ride systems, virtual queue rollouts, app features from parks)
- Weekly/Monthly Roundups (digest-style summaries of everything that happened across the industry)
- Rumors & Speculation (patent filings, permit applications, aerial construction photos — clearly labeled as unconfirmed)

## 2. Park Guides
- Time-Constrained Visit Plans (2-hour, half-day, full-day, evening-only itineraries factoring in day of week and season)
- Food & Drink Deep Dives (best beverages, secret combos, snack tier lists, sit-down meal reviews, seasonal exclusives, hidden menu items, "worth the price" breakdowns)
- Ride Strategy & Optimization (best ride order with and without fast pass, underrated flats, skip-unless-short-line lists, time-of-day line patterns)
- Seasonal & Event Guides (dedicated guides per event — maze rankings, scare zone routes, festival-exclusive food worth trying)
- Money-Saving & Logistics (parking strategy, ticket deal timing, dining plan value analysis, what to bring vs buy inside, fast pass worth-it analysis by day)
- Hidden Gems & Easter Eggs (park history baked into theming, photo ops most people miss, overlooked experiences, lore deep cuts)
- Families & Accessibility (toddler-friendly plans, mixed-age group strategies, honest accessibility info beyond the park website, quiet decompression spots, restraint comfort notes)
- First-Timer vs Return Visitor (obvious must-dos for newbies, "you've been three times and you're still missing this" for returning guests)
- Comparisons & Versus (park vs park for a day trip, event vs event like Scary Farm vs HHN, value comparisons)
- Photo & Content Creator Spots (best photo locations, golden hour timing per area, on-ride photo tips, Instagram-worthy food presentation)

## 3. Historical Deep Dives
- Defunct Ride Retrospectives (full story of rides that no longer exist — why built, what made them special, why removed)
- Legendary Designers & Engineers (Arrow's Ron Toomer, B&M's founders, Anton Schwarzkopf — philosophy and body of work)
- Park Origin Stories (how parks went from farmland to what they are today — Knott's berry farm origins, Cedar Point's beach resort era)
- Manufacturer Histories (rise and fall of Arrow, Vekoma's redemption arc, Intamin's reputation, RMC's revolution)
- Ride Type Evolution (how woodies evolved, history of the inversion, launch coaster technology progression, dark rides simple to immersive)
- Lost Parks & Forgotten Attractions (Astroworld, Geauga Lake, Hard Rock Park — what happened and what's left)
- Record Breakers Through the Decades (how "tallest" and "fastest" records have been shattered, the arms race eras)
- Behind the Scenes of Iconic Builds (construction stories, engineering challenges, things that went wrong or almost didn't happen)

## 4. Culture Pieces
- Credit Counting Culture (what it means, the debate, notable credit counters, the psychology of collecting)
- Enthusiast Community Events (coaster conventions, ERT events, club meetups — what they're like, how to get involved)
- Thoosie Vocabulary & Inside Jokes (GP vs enthusiast perspective, what "forceless" really means, memes and running gags)
- YouTube & Content Creator Spotlights (profiles on notable coaster YouTubers, how coaster content creation became its own niche)
- The GP vs Enthusiast Divide (how enthusiasts and GP experience parks completely differently, why enthusiasts argue about trims)
- Coaster Fashion & Merch Culture (battle vests with park patches, pin trading, coaster-themed apparel brands)
- Online Communities Deep Dives (Reddit's r/rollercoasters, Theme Park Review forums, CoasterBuzz — each community's personality)
- International Enthusiast Culture (how the hobby differs in Europe, Asia, South America — different priorities, different favorites)
- Controversies & Hot Takes (debates that split the community — RMC conversions of classic woodies, virtual queue systems, upcharge culture)

## 5. Ride Reviews
- Tier 1 Marquee Reviews (full deep-dive: ride experience breakdown, best seat analysis, queue walkthrough, historical context, enthusiast vs GP perspective, POV embed)
- Tier 2 Short Takes (2-3 paragraph reviews for mid-tier rides: what to expect, one key tip, one interesting fact)
- Seat-by-Seat Comparisons (front vs back vs middle on specific rides — when it actually matters and when it doesn't)
- Re-Ride Reassessments (revisiting after retrack, new trains, or change in opinion — did GhostRider get better or did you just get used to it)
- Ride Comparisons Within a Type (best B&M invert showdown, RMC conversions ranked, top launch coasters head to head)
- Night Ride vs Day Ride (rides that genuinely transform after dark — where the experience is materially different)
- New-For-This-Year Reviews (timely reviews of coasters that just opened — first impressions backed by multiple ride research)

## 6. Trip Reports
- Single Park Day Recaps (one park, one day — what they rode, ate, what surprised them, crowd levels)
- Multi-Park Road Trip Reports (several parks — logistics, highlights, route strategy)
- Event-Specific Reports (Scary Farm opening weekend, Boysenberry Festival day one, specific ERT event)
- "I Went On a Tuesday in February" Reports (off-peak visit reports — walk-ons, closed rides, different vibe)
- International Park Reports (visiting parks abroad — cultural differences, language barriers, logistics tips)
- Then vs Now Revisits (returning after years — what changed, what's better, what's worse, nostalgia vs reality)
- Challenge & Themed Visits (marathon sessions, every-ride-in-the-park attempts, speed runs, food challenges)

## 7. Seasonal Content
- Opening Day Coverage (what's new, crowd reports, ride availability, surprises)
- Haunt & Halloween Event Guides (maze rankings, scare zone breakdowns, survival tips, food exclusives, park comparisons)
- Holiday Overlay Coverage (Christmas events, winter festivals, what's worth seeing vs skippable)
- Off-Season Updates (construction, removals, refurbishments, announcements during closed months)
- Season Pass & Membership Analysis (when to buy, tier breakdown, perks, value calculation by visit frequency)
- Water Park & Summer Specials (summer events, water rides, beat-the-heat strategies, extended hours)
- End-of-Season Retrospectives (how the season went — best additions, biggest disappointments, what to watch next year)

## 8. Buyer's Guides & Gear
- What to Wear (shoes that survive 10 hours, what not to wear on inversions)
- Bags & Pouches (best bags for no-loose-articles policies)
- Camera & POV Gear (phone mounts, action cameras for POV filming)
- Sun Protection & Hydration (sunscreen, water bottles, cooling gear)
- "What's in My Park Bag" Breakdowns
- Natural affiliate revenue fit (Undercover Tourist and beyond)

## 9. Rankings & Lists
- Best Coasters by Type (best wooden, best invert, best launch)
- Best Coasters by Region
- Most Underrated Rides
- Most Overrated Rides
- Best Parks for Specific Audiences (families, thrill-seekers, foodies)
- Community-Voted Rankings (aggregated opinions from forums and YouTube polls, not just one perspective)

## 10. Industry & Behind the Curtain
- How Ride Maintenance Works
- What a Coaster Engineer Actually Does
- How Parks Decide What to Build Next
- The Economics of a New Coaster (cost vs attendance bump)
- How Theming Budgets Work
- Ride Technology Explainers (how magnetic brakes work, what makes an RMC feel different)

---

## Pipeline Note

Each subcategory needs its own adapted version of the research prompt template (see `research-flow.md`). The source priority ladder shifts per subcategory:
- Food deep dives → YouTube/TikTok food reviewers + park menus
- Historical deep dives → archived articles + enthusiast wikis + Coasterpedia
- Trip report aggregations → Reddit + forum posts + vlogs
- News → Screamscape + trade press + official park announcements
- Gear → product review sites + Amazon + enthusiast forums

Build prompt templates PER SUBCATEGORY for consistent quality at scale.
