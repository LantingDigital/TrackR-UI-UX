# Source Image Verification (NanoBanana Pipeline)

## The Rule

Before submitting ANY source image to Gemini NanoBanana, verify:

1. **Correct coaster.** The image must show the ACTUAL coaster listed, not a different ride at a different park. Cross-reference against RCDB (rcdb.com) to confirm the coaster's appearance.
2. **Correct park.** Verify the park name matches. Many coasters share names across parks.
3. **No copyrighted characters.** Promotional renders with Disney/Marvel/Universal characters will be BLOCKED by Gemini's content policy. Use fan photos or press images without characters.
4. **Outdoor coasters:** Show clear track detail (close-up shots preferred over whole-ride views).
5. **Indoor/enclosed coasters:** Use station shots or car-in-station photos. The train/car is enough for NanoBanana to work with.

## Why

Session 2026-03-14: 3 of 10 source images were completely wrong coasters (Avengers showed a Brazilian wild mouse, Ba-A-A Express showed Europa-Park's dome, Anubis showed a Chinese "Happy Time" building). None of these were caught before submission, wasting generation cycles and producing unusable cards.

## Indoor / Enclosed Coasters (CRITICAL)

Indoor and enclosed coasters (e.g., Black Diamond, Space Mountain, Rock 'n' Roller Coaster) will NOT have visible outdoor track. For these:
- **ALWAYS use a station photo showing the train/car in the station.** There is almost always one available on RCDB or fan sites.
- A building facade photo is NOT sufficient -- NanoBanana will just reimagine the building, which isn't useful card art.
- The train/car in station gives NanoBanana enough to work with for a compelling card.

## How to Verify

- Check RCDB page for the coaster ID to see what it actually looks like
- Compare source image against RCDB photos
- If the source image doesn't clearly match, replace it before submitting
- For indoor rides, specifically search for "station" or "train" photos
